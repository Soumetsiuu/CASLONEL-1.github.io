My introduction

Caslonel is an guide for commuters that use LRT1 to travel. Caslonel will give information to commuters to follow for them to be able to get to their designated destination of choice. Caslonel will provide the following information such as Station to go, Estimated travel time, Price, Nearby Landmarks and many more.
